Here at Garden XL, we’ve been busy getting our garden ready for the occasion.
Strawberries and cream are a truly British combination. The combination has roots
in the Tudor court, first being served by Cardinal Wolsey and has become
synonymous with the legendary tennis tournament.

We’ve taken inspiration from the dessert and will be cheering on the players from our
strawberry and cream themed Bracken Outdoors Caymen furniture set.
We’ve paired it with our red Bracken Outdoors Shanghai parasol for a perfectly
patriotic garden look.
